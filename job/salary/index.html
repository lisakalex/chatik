What to Expect

For a DevOps Engineer with ~3 years experience in the South East UK:

  • Typical salary range: £55,000 – £70,000/year

  • Established mid-level range: £60,000 – £75,000

  • High-end roles (especially in London-proximate or tech-heavy sectors): up to £80,000 – £85,000+

Tips for Positioning Yourself

  1. Benchmark locally — use LinkedIn, Glassdoor, or recruitment sites to compare with similar roles (e.g., Reading, Thames Valley).

  2. Highlight your specialisations — tools like Terraform, Kubernetes, AWS/Azure, CI/CD pipeline automation tend to attract premium pay.

  3. Negotiate based on data — arm yourself with these salary figures during interviews or reviews.

  4. Consider cost-of-living vs. commute — roles near London often offer higher pay, but may come with higher living or travel costs if not remote.

With three years under your belt, it's reasonable to expect somewhere between £60 k and £75 k in the South East UK. If you're earning in the high £40ks, you're catching up—but still within the acceptable range for some contexts.
